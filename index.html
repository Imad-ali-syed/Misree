About Misree Chai

A neighborhood cafe with a premium soul.

Misree Chai is designed around comfort, quality, and visual elegance. We serve handcrafted chai, refined desserts, and cafe favorites in a soft cream-and-brown setting that invites guests to slow down, connect, and enjoy every moment.

Experience

  • Handcrafted chai recipes with premium ingredients
  • Aesthetic interiors for relaxed meetups and cozy evenings
  • Carefully selected dessert pairings
  • Local neighborhood warmth with elevated presentation
