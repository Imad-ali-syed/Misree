About Misree Chai

A neighborhood cafe with a premium soul.

Misree Chai blends boutique-cafe aesthetics with crowd-pleasing drinks and desserts. The experience is simple: beautiful presentation, rich flavors, a welcoming vibe, and a menu broad enough for tea lovers, dessert lovers, and casual cafe visitors alike.

What people come for

  • Bubble tea in classic, fruit, and kulfi-inspired flavors
  • Acai bowls, mocktails, bakery items, and crepes
  • Comfort drinks like Irani chai, masala chai, and South Indian coffee
  • Warm aesthetic and a polished neighborhood-cafe experience
